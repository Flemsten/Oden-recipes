Description

Noodles with chicken and sweet chilli sauce.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Chicken
  • Sweet chilli sauce

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken length size in to strips.
  2. Start boiling the water.
  3. Start frying the chicken.
  4. When the chicken has started to get color, pour the seet chilli sauce over it.
  5. When the noodles finisht boiling, drain them and dump them in the pan with the chicken.
  6. Toss it around and then plate.

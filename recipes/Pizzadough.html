Description

A simple pizzadough.

Ingredienta

  • Flour
  • Oil
  • Yeast
  • Water

Steps

  1. Start by disolving the yeast in the water.
  2. Then add the flour to the water.
  3. Add the oil in.
  4. Start mixing and neading the dough until you can see a gluten window through it.
  5. Let the dough rest for an hour.
